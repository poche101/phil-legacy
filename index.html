About Phil Legacy

Phil Legacy Nig. Ltd is a distinguished Nigerian trading and export company, specializing in premium agricultural products.

Founded in 2019, we have rapidly positioned ourselves as a trusted global supplier, delivering high-quality agro commodities from Nigeria to markets around the world. With a focus on excellence, sustainability, and reliability, we cater to the growing demand for premium agro products while maintaining strong relationships with farmers, processors, and global buyers.

Our commitment to quality and ethical sourcing ensures that every product we deliver meets international standards. We pride ourselves on being a key player in promoting Nigeria's agricultural potential on the global stage while supporting the livelihoods of local farmers through sustainable and fair trade practices.
