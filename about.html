About Phil Legacy

Phil Legacy Nig. Ltd is a distinguished Nigerian trading and export company, specializing in premium agricultural products. Founded in 2019, we have rapidly positioned ourselves as a trusted global supplier, delivering high-quality agro commodities from Nigeria to markets around the world.

Our commitment to quality and ethical sourcing ensures that every product we deliver meets international standards. We pride ourselves on being a key player in promoting Nigeria's agricultural potential on the global stage.
